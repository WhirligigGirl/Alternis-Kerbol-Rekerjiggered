**Alternis_OPM:** A more in-depth support config for Outer Planets Mod, more in line with Alternis Kerbol.
	* Moves Neidon inward to be a Hot Neptune, gives it a new texture.
	* Shrinks Wal slightly. Moves Wal and Tal to orbit Tylo.
	* Increases Sarnus gravity to 1.9g, with a slightly larger radius to boot.
	* Shrinks Slate.
	* Makes Urlum a proper 1/10th scale analogue of Uranus
	* Moves Neidon's moons to orbit Urlum
	* Plock and Alternis Eeloo systems moved in to be relative to Urlum instead of Neidon.

**Alternis_Parallax:** Support for Parallax Continued.
	* NOTE: you must go to the terrain quality settings and select the right-most setting, "AlternisParallax" in order to set the subdivision levels correctly.
	* Install only ParallaxContinued, Parallax_StockTerrainTextures, and Parallax_StockScatterTextures.
	* DO NOT install Parallax_StockPlanetTextures under any circumstances.
	* When you uninstall the mod, make sure you reset your terrain quality settings and/or delete your ksp's Settings.cfg.
	* Gauranteed to be at least a little janky.
	* Tested with Sigma Dimensions 2.56x rescale only, because that's how I was playing Alternis when I made it.
	* No warranty.
	* Tylo scatters and terrain changed to improve performance
	* Laythe given most of Kerbin's scatters, but with one subtle difference.
	* Minmus made properly red

**AlternisTechTree:** The tech tree I'm playing with in my current (April 2026) Alternis 2.56x scale playthough.
	* Designed to be played with ChemicalPropulsion, AlcoholicAeronautics, and ReStock/ReStockPlus.
	* Increases the mass and volume a Kerbal can carry so that she can hold a jetpack and surface science at the same time.
	* Replaces the Wolfhound, which I hate, with the Schnauzer, when ReStockPlus is installed and ChemicalPropulsion is not installed.
	* Move Sepratron and Stayputnik to start node.
	* When a given size category of fuel tank is unlocked, you unlock all lengths of that tank.
	* This is support with and without AlcoholicAeronautics's tech tree changes.
	* Swap the LV-T30 and LV-T45--the latter should be higher tech.
	* Move the stack bi-, tri-, and quad-couplers to Stability, where they can do some good. No use using them after you've unlocked 1.875m and 2.5m parts, after all.
	* Make fairings unlock much earlier.
	* Give you a powerful relay in basicScience for early game interplanetary probes.
	* Move the 1.875m service bay to unlock with the MH service modules.
